Conference Registration

Click Here for 2018 Conference Registration

Registration to the conference includes:

  1.  Attendance at conference plenary, technical paper sessions and workshop tutorials
  2.  Exhibition of sponsoring companies
  3.  Light refreshments during coffee breaks
  4.  Parking
  5.  WiFi access during the conference
  6.  Conference proceedings (accepted papers) and workshop materials in electronic form
  7.  Chance to win prizes
  8.  Inclusion of paper in IEEE Xplore (for authors of accepted papers)
  9.  Chance to win best student poster award (for students enrolling in poster competition)
Baylor University will host both the Dinner on Thursday and the lunch on Friday at the conference venue and all conference attendees are invited (regardless of registration rate).
